Top Hospitality jobs in Australia

Blog article

I wrote an article for PuraVida Study, an Australian students agency based in Sydney. I lived in Australia under a Working Holiday Visa scheme from 2018 until the end of 2019 and once I came back to Argentina, during the pandemic in the year 2020 I collaborated with the agency sharing my experience and knowledge about the country, the typical jobs for backpackers: waitress, hotel receptionist, restaurant hostess, bartenders, among others from the Front of House category. For those whose English skills were not enough to work in customer service the ideal jobs were dishwasher, chef, kitchen hand and cleaning.

Top 5 things to visit in Broome

Blog article

Broome is a paradise. I would say, the hidden pearl of the West. It’s a small town on the West Coast of Australia, north of the Tropic of Capricorn. As the region has tropical weather, the best time to visit it is from June to September during the dry season. On these months the temperature is just perfect with up to 30°C on daytime and cool nights of no less than 15°C. On this blog article I share my experience and the stunning places this little town has to offer.
